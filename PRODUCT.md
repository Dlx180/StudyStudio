# Product

## Register

product

## Users

KnowTree serves university students, graduate students, researchers, and self-learners who read PDFs, slides, papers, and course notes while trying to build a durable understanding of the material. They are usually in a focused study session and need the source document, structure, progress, and AI assistance to stay aligned.

## Product Purpose

KnowTree is an AI-assisted learning material manager. Its MVP helps a learner read the original material directly while an editable UnitTree keeps the learning structure, current unit, page range, state, and next action visible. Success means the learner trusts the source, understands where they are in the material, and feels steadily accompanied instead of pushed through a generic chat-with-PDF flow.

## Brand Personality

Calm, attentive, and capable. The product should feel like a quiet study companion: personally aware, never clingy, and precise enough to be trusted during serious learning work.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI SaaS landing pages, purple-gradient assistant aesthetics, loud productivity dashboards, gamified streak pressure, and chat interfaces that make the AI feel more important than the original material.

## Design Principles

- Keep the original material primary and trusted.
- Make the UnitTree feel like a readable path, not a rigid task list.
- Use companion copy to reduce friction and anxiety without becoming sentimental.
- Prefer restrained product UI patterns that support repeated study sessions.
- Surface progress and state as gentle orientation, not performance pressure.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard-accessible controls. Maintain readable text on small screens, visible focus states, and layouts that work for learners who need slower reading, reduced motion, or clear state labels beyond color alone.
